A Deep Dive into Core Finance

Core Finance was built from the ground up to provide a comprehensive yet easy-to-use solution for financial tracking. Whether you're an individual managing a household budget, a freelancer tracking project income, or a small business owner overseeing expenses and revenues, our application is designed to adapt to your needs.

Who is Core Finance for?

  • Individuals & Families: Keep a close eye on your household budget, track monthly spending across categories like food and housing, and plan for future goals.
  • Freelancers & Self-Employed Professionals: Manage project-based income and expenses, separate business from personal finances, and generate reports for tax season.
  • Small Business Owners: Use the "Enterprise Mode" to manage clients and suppliers, track accounts receivable and payable, and understand your company's cash flow with detailed reports.

Core Features in Detail

  • Dual Modes: Switch seamlessly between "Personal Mode" for simplified tracking and "Enterprise Mode" to access advanced features like contact management.
  • Comprehensive Dashboards: Visualize your financial situation with charts for expenses by category, income vs. expenses, aging reports, and more.
  • Secure Local Backups: Perform manual or automatic backups of your data. Since all data is stored on your machine, you have full control.
  • Customization: Personalize the application with dark/light themes and custom backgrounds.
  • Notes with Audio: Attach detailed notes to your profiles, complete with audio recordings—perfect for reminders or logging transaction details.

Your Privacy is Paramount

In an era of data breaches, Core Finance takes a different approach. The application is built to work entirely offline. No data is ever sent to or stored on our servers. It all resides on your local computer, encrypted and secure. You are in 100% control, always.

Privacy Policy for Core Finance

Last Updated: September 24, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Core Finance handles your information. Our commitment is to your privacy, and our policy is simple: we do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or financial data.

1. No Data Collection

Core Finance is a desktop application that operates entirely offline. All data you input into the application, including but not limited to transactions, categories, contacts, notes, audio recordings, and profile information, is stored exclusively on your local computer's hard drive. Your data is never sent to our servers or any third-party servers.

2. Local Storage

All your information is saved in your local storage, within the application's designated data path on your operating system. You have full control over this data. The security and maintenance of the computer on which the software is installed are the user's responsibility.
